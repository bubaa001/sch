About Francis Maria Libermann School

Discover our rich history, mission, and commitment to holistic education in Zanzibar.

Our History

Foundation

Welcome to Francis Maria Libermann School in which was founded in 1994. It is doing well academically as well as good morals. The school is located just 5km from Stone Town. Our School offers education to all, from nursery level to Secondary level. Francis Maria Libermann School is a Catholic School owned by the Catholic Diocese of Zanzibar. The School is situated in the Shehia of Tomondo, in western district, in urban western Region. The School started in 1996 with Nursery School followed by Primary School in 1998. Secondary School level followed in 2007. The School embraces all people who wish to fetch the suitable education offered.

Growth and Development

Over the years, we have expanded our facilities and programs to meet the growing needs of our students and community. Our commitment to academic excellence and character formation has remained steadfast.

Today, we continue to uphold our motto 'Education Key to Liberty' while adapting to modern educational demands.

Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To Provide Quality Education with Competitive Standards, to help the Students Become more Curious, Reflective and Critical Thinkers as Global citizens.

Our Vision

To develop Learners who are: Confident in working with their information, ideas and those of others; Responsible for themselves, environment and respect of others; Reflective as learners, developing their ability to learn; Innovative and equipped for new and future challenges; Engaged intellectually, spiritually and socially and ready to make a difference.
