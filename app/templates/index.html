About Us

Our History

Marist Boys Secondary School was established on May 10, 2013, by the Marist Brothers in Nyakato ward, Ilemela Municipal Council, Mwanza, Tanzania. Starting with 84 students, the school has grown to offer education from Form 1 to Form 6, embodying the Marist ethos of holistic development.

Located near the Sayona area (FX88+G7F), our school is a Catholic institution welcoming boys of all religious backgrounds, focusing on academic excellence and character formation under the motto 'Memento ascendere semper' (Remember always to aim higher).

Our Mission and Vision

Rooted in the values of the Marist Brothers, we provide a holistic education that nurtures intellectual, moral, and spiritual growth. Our mission is to empower boys to become responsible, self-reliant individuals who contribute positively to society.

We emphasize community engagement through activities like singing and educational visits, such as to Sayona Drinking Company, fostering practical learning and a sense of service. Our academic programs are supported by facilities like science labs, libraries, and sports fields.
